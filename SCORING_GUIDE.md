# Berrycom Supply Chain Maturity Model (SCMM) - Question Scoring Guide

This guide helps you accurately assess your organization's supply chain maturity level for each question. For each question, find the description that best matches your current state.

## Scoring Scale
- **Level 1 - Ad-Hoc/Reactive**: No structured process, firefighting mode
- **Level 2 - Functional/Emerging**: Basic processes exist but are siloed 
- **Level 3 - Integrated/Process-Driven**: Cross-functional coordination and integration
- **Level 4 - Optimized/Data-Driven**: Advanced analytics and optimization
- **Level 5 - Transformational/Digital**: AI-driven, best-in-class, digital twins

## How to Use This Guide

1. **For each question, read through the level descriptions**
2. **Find the description that best matches your current state**
3. **Select the corresponding level (1-5) in the assessment**
4. **Be honest about your current capabilities, not aspirational goals**
5. **Consider providing evidence or examples to support your rating**

## Tips for Accurate Assessment

- **Focus on current state**, not future plans
- **Consider the majority of your operations**, not exceptional cases
- **Involve multiple stakeholders** for cross-functional perspectives
- **Document your rationale** for future reference and improvement tracking
- **Review your assessment** before finalizing to ensure consistency

This scoring guide ensures accurate self-assessment and provides a clear roadmap for improvement at each maturity level.
